My Data Story

Welcome!

Hi and welcome to my data story! Throughout the course of ENGL 3844 at Virginia Tech, I have been learning about my data consumption and then looking into how that plays into the data consumption of our society. I am a lover of podcasts. Thus, I decided to explore how, when, and what podcasts I am listening to during one week. Enjoy!

Personal Data Consumption

Additionally, I highlight how I feel after I listen to a podcast. Thus, what I am listening to can impact how I am feeling throughout the day.
